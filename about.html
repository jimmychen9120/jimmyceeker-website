Jimmy Chen

Hi, my name is Jimmy! I was born and raised in the northwest suburbs of Chicago and currently an undergraduate student at Marquette University up in Milwaukee studying Philosophy and Computer Science.


I started my endeavor into professional visual arts at the start of 2019 -- after a desire to make films as a young, creative child -- with photography. Over the years, I've expanded my skillset with videography, photo editing, and video editing. After years of writing through different mediums (poetry, fiction, articles), I began screenwriting in 2020 in order to do story-based filmmaking and more exciting personal passion projects. Along with entering scriptwriting contests, I've opened up doing freelance work for local business and artists for their photography, videography, and social media needs.


I started JimmyCeeker as a platform to show my creative endeavors and growth so that people can enjoy some of the content I make. I would really appreciate you taking a look at some of my work and consider me for your next visual media needs.

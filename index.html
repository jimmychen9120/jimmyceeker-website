Who is JimmyCeeker?

JimmyCeeker is the creative channel for which Jimmy Chen expresses his artistic vision. Jimmy is a Chicago and Milwaukee based photographer, filmmaker, and writer. Storytelling is Jimmy's passion and he strives to put his own artistic perspective with every photograph, script, and video/film he makes. Even for client work, Jimmy incorporates the clientele's brand and mission with his storytelling vision to execute and deliver a professional media package. Jimmy's discipline and work ethic, combined with his artistic perspective, makes him a creative force that produces high quality content.
